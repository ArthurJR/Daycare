Infant

The infant class is a place for our youngest students to start their development path. With a low ratio of students to teachers, individual attention is constantly giving to your child throughout the day. “Tummy Time”, trust building exercises, and environment discovery is explored here. Soothing music is played throughout the day and a flexible feeding, nap, and changing schedule caters to you and your child’s needs. Very detailed daily sheets are filled out and drop off and continue through to pick up.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:4

1 Year Old

The One Year Old class is a place for our emerging toddlers to begin environment mastery, object recognition, and the beginning of social interaction. Once the child is placed in this class, teachers encourage the children to walk constantly, develop speech mastery, and begin emotional training. A flexible schedule is implemented in this class and daily sheets are filled for each event throughout their stay.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:6

2 Year Old

The Two Year Old class is a place for our toddlers to strengthen speech skills, master group play, and begin stages of emotional intelligence. Knowing the difference between right and wrong and adhering to a more structured schedule are our milestones. Once in this class, learning to drink from cups and potty training is also implemented. Daily sheets are provided in this class on a general outlook of the day.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:11

3 Year Old

The Three Year Old class is a place for our emerging kids to master speech, master emotional and social interaction, and begin stages of learning about self. This class has the most developmental growth as cognitive skills grow exponentially. Structured play and educational activities are given daily. We would need children to be working on being potty trained. Daily sheets are provided in this class on a general outlook of the day.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:14

4 Year Old

The Four Year Old class is a place for our youths to acquire the skills to begin school. Structured learning activities and school based schedules are implemented in this class. Mastery of Self and language are major milestones in this class.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:14

VPK

The VPK class is a state readiness preparation class. VPK stands for Voluntary Pre-Kindergarten. Children in this class acquire the skills to start kindergarten. As in the 4 Year Old Class, structured learning activities and school based schedules are implemented in this class but an emphasis on communal play, imaginative discovery, and mastery of cognition are milestones before moving to school. Children must be turning 4 by September 1st to be placed in this class.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:11

After Care (5 year Old-8 Year Old)

Our After Care class is a place for our school aged kids to wind down and have fun after school as they await the conclusion of their parent’s work days. Art/crafts, sports, and homework/school review are done in this class. This class is active as enrollment grows.

Teacher to Student Ratio is 1:14
